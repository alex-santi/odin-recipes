Shrimp Marinara

Description

A classic italian recipe: The shrimp marinara!
A red marinara sauce combined with shrimp and served over pasta. The perfect dish for a nice and classy dinner.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 12oz of dry spaghetti noodles.
  • Oil: 3 tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil.
  • Shrimp: 1 pound of peeled and deveined large shrimp.
  • Seasoning:
    • 4 cloves of sliced garlic.
    • 1/2 teaspoon of crushed red pepper.
    • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil.
    • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano.
    • 1/4 tablespoon kosher salt.
  • Canned tomatoes: 1 (15oz) diced fire-roasted tomatoes and 1 (15oz) canned tomato sauce.
  • Balsamic vinegar: A tablespoon of balsamic vinegar.

Steps

  1. Clean your cooking area and gather all the ingredients.

  2. Cook your spaghetti by following the package instructions; drain the water and put it to the side.

  3. In the meantime, heat some oil on a large skillet at medium heat. Add your shrimp and cook for 2 to 3 minutes or until opaque.
    Make sure to turn it and get the other side.

  4. Transfer the shimp to a plate. Add garlic and crushed red pepper to the skillet. Cook and stir until fragrant, aprox 1 min.

  5. Stir undrained tomatoes, tomato suace, basil, balsamic vinegar, oregano, and salt. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, until thickened, 10 min.

  6. Return the shrimp to the skillet and simmer to heat them through, apox 1 min. Serve over spaghetti, topped with additional fresh basil.
